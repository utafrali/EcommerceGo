# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all contributors, regardless of experience level, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or nationality.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, trolling, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate corrective action in response to any unacceptable behavior.

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the project maintainers directly.
